Build a Brighter Future with OL Afrika Media

Here at OL Afrika Media Foundation, we are committed to a single goal; we want to make the world a better place. We work hard to take care of the needs of our arts community and provide them with unconditional support. Founded in 2016, OL Afrika Media Foundation has been driven by the same ideas we initially founded our Arts Organization upon: we want to see pieces of change in this crazy world we live in. Read about our mission and our vision and find out how we go about making change.

  • Providing unconditional support to the arts community.
  • Working towards a better world through our mission and vision.
  • Bringing positive change to the world through our work.
